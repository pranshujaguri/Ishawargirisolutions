FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the service area of Ishwar Giri Solution India?
We primarily serve Ghaziabad, Noida, Delhi NCR, Faridabad and Meerut. For large-scale projects — transformer erection, substation work and DG rentals — we also operate pan-India. Contact us to discuss your project location.
Do you offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)?
Yes. We offer comprehensive AMC plans for Transformers (11KV & 33KV), DG Sets up to 1500 KVA, HT/LT Panels, and all electrical equipment. AMC includes periodic servicing, testing and priority breakdown support.
Do you provide emergency breakdown support?
Yes. We provide 24×7 emergency breakdown support for critical electrical equipment. Call us immediately on 8882362092 for urgent assistance.
Can you rent DG Sets for temporary or event use?
Yes. Through our United Power Solutions partnership, we rent DG Sets up to 1500 KVA for industrial, construction, event and emergency power requirements. We also rent 11KV and 33KV transformers for temporary power needs.
Are you a Govt. approved contractor?
Yes. Ishwar Giri (Solution India) is a Government Approved A-Class Electrical Contractor, which means we are authorised to undertake high-tension (HT) electrical works including substation installations, HT panels and transformer erection.
